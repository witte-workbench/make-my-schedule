How do I use it?

In Make My Schedule, type in your term and class names. Use settings and sliders to fine-tune your preferences. Then, click "Make Schedules!"
Your school's registration site will open, all the classes available will be read automatically. Every possible schedule will be generated, and sorted to your liking. You can flip through options with the number boxes on top.
When you find one you like, click "Save Schedule". Now, you can view, register, and plan this schedule.
Note: MMS is student made and not officially supported by Ellucian nor any schools.
